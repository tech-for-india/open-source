Mission

Empowering children through technology and education

"The destiny of a nation is being shaped in its classrooms."
Education Commission, 1966

Our Purpose: Use technology to empower children denied equal access to education.

Our Vision:

  • Build open-source educational tools that reach every corner of the nation.
  • Bridge the digital divide with free, multilingual, lightweight software.
  • Show that nation-building can happen alongside our daily jobs—one line of code at a time.
